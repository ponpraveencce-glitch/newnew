How It Works

Securing credentials in four transparent cryptographic steps.

1

Certificate Issued

Academic details are inputted and approved by authority.

2

Stored Securely

Metadata records are stored in the local registry database.

3

Hash Generated

A unique SHA-256 cryptographic signature is mined into the blockchain ledger.

4

Certificate Verified

Employers check validity instantly via PDF uploads or Certificate ID queries.

Why Blockchain?

Our decentralized architecture makes certificate falsification impossible.

Tamper Proof

Any modification to certificate details instantly invalidates the blockchain hash signature matching checks.

Secure

Cryptographic locks seal student achievements into blocks, guaranteeing absolute data authenticity.

Transparent

Every transaction block carries timestamp verification logs visible to any auditor without registration.

Decentralized

Eliminates dependency on single central databases, validating proofs directly against the ledger hashes.

About BlockCert

BlockCert is a next-generation academic credentialing registry designed to eliminate credential fraud. By leveraging cryptographic hashing and decentralized verification workflows, it allows colleges, universities, and technical academies to publish verified accomplishments that are instantly audit-ready.

Our platform generates cryptographically signed PDF certificates containing embedded SHA-256 integrity proofs. Third parties can cross-reference the certificates in a fraction of a second, preventing the distribution of counterfeit diplomas and certifications.
